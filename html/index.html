Reversi
Reversi Board Implementation

The implementation creates an 8 X 8 Reversi board and places players' pieces in the center as per the default configuration.

The user has the ability to set its pieces at a particular cell on the board.

If the move is legal, the board sets the piece and flips the required intermediate pieces in compliance with the game rules.

Game rules

A player can capture their opponent's pieces by trapping them between two of their own pieces horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

When a player makes a move that results in capturing opponent pieces, those pieces are changed to the capturing player's color.

In order for the move to be deemed legal, at least one opponent's piece must be captured.
